## Sales Process

### Lead Qualification
<Steps>
  **Trigger**
  NeatAudit completion or partner referral

  **Automated Triage**
  NeatLM analyzes and assigns readiness tier

  **Routing**
  Automated workflow based on tier classification

  **Engagement**
  Tailored follow-up based on readiness score
</Steps>

### Readiness Tiers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Deploy Now" icon="rocket">
    - High confidence score
    - Clear ROI potential
    - Immediate consultation
  </Card>
  <Card title="Nurture" icon="seedling">
    - Moderate score
    - Needs education
    - Automated sequence
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monitor" icon="eye">
    - Low score
    - Poor fit currently
    - Light-touch content
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Conversion Process
<Steps>
  **Initial Assessment**
  Client completes NeatAudit

  **Automated Analysis**
  NeatLM processes data and generates roadmap

  **Consultation**
  Focused discussion for qualified leads

  **Proposal**
  Roadmap acceptance and project initiation

  **Onboarding**
  Neat Digital project kickoff and Portal access
</Steps>
