## Market Size

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
<Card title="Australian SME Sector" icon="chart-pie">
- Over 2.5 million businesses (97.4% of all active firms)
- SME digital transformation spending projected to exceed $16B AUD annually by 2026
- Global AI consulting and implementation market for small businesses expected to reach $200B USD by 2030
</Card>

### Serviceable Available Market (SAM)
<Card title="Target SME Segment" icon="bullseye">
- Australian SMEs in services, healthcare, manufacturing, and professional sectors
- 5-100 employees (approximately 650,000 businesses)
- Current spending of $4.2B AUD on software, process optimization, and technical consulting
</Card>

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
<Card title="Initial Target" icon="target">
- Estimated 6,000 clients within 3 years
- Just under 1% of SAM through NeatAudit and downstream services
- Approximately $18-24M AUD in direct audit and implementation revenue
</Card>
