## Competitive Landscape Overview

<Card title="Market Structure" icon="chess-board">
The competitive landscape in AI consulting and SME-focused automation is fragmented and emerging. While enterprise-level platforms dominate the AI infrastructure market, there is a notable absence of vertically integrated solutions purpose-built for SMEs like the SrvdNeat ecosystem.
</Card>

### Market Bifurcation

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Large Consulting Firms" icon="building">
    Firms like Accenture, Deloitte, PwC offer end-to-end AI strategy services, but are prohibitively expensive and misaligned with SME needs
  </Card>
  <Card title="SaaS Automation Tools" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Tools like Zapier, Make, Airtable provide building blocks but require self-diagnosis and integration - lacking strategic guidance
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
